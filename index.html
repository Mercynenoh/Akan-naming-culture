Brief about the Akan culture

The Akan culture is on of the most popular cultures in Africa. It is practiced in Ghana. The Akan people are about 47% of the total population. Infact, it has even spread to other places including the carribean, south and north America. The names are usually given depending on the day of the week when you were born.
